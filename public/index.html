FeatureIQ applies one transparent scoring model to your entire JIRA backlog — so every priority call rests on the same criteria, and every stakeholder can see exactly why it was made.

The scoring model
transparent by design
I
User Impact
How significantly will this improve user experience or solve a real pain point, scored against your acceptance criteria and user stories.
II
Engineering Effort
The engineering cost, estimated on the Fibonacci scale and reconciled against your team's story points and velocity.
III
Strategic Alignment
How well does this align with your stated team priorities, epics, and business goals for the quarter.
IV
Risk
Dependencies, compliance concerns, and technical uncertainty — flagged before they become sprint blockers.
✓
Weighted to your team's goals, and fully auditable — every score traces back to its inputs. Powered by Claude AI.
