Enterprise feature prioritisation

One transparent model. Every decision explained.

FeatureIQ applies the same four criteria to your entire backlog — so every priority call rests on the same footing, and every stakeholder can see exactly why it was made.

The scoring model

transparent by design
  1. I
    User Impact

    How significantly this improves the user experience or solves a real pain point, scored against your acceptance criteria and user stories.

  2. II
    Engineering Effort

    The engineering cost, estimated on the Fibonacci scale and reconciled against your team's story points and velocity.

  3. III
    Strategic Alignment

    How well the work supports your stated team priorities, epics, and business goals for the quarter.

  4. IV
    Risk

    Dependencies, compliance concerns, and technical uncertainty — flagged before they become sprint blockers.

✓

Weighted to your team's goals, and fully auditable — every score traces back to its inputs. Powered by Claude.
